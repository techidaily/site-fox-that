1. Go to the**Settings** app.

## 3\. Turn Off Silent Mode and Do Not Disturb

 It‚Äôs convenient to use Silent Mode and Do Not Disturb to focus at work. Therefore, a simple reason why there‚Äôs no sound on your iPhone could be that these features are still turned on.

Here‚Äôs how to turn off Silent Mode:

1. Find the**Ring/Silent** switch, located on the top-left side of your iPhone.
2. If Silent Mode is on, an orange strip shows.
3. Move the switch towards the screen to cover the orange strip. This turns off Silent Mode.

Here‚Äôs how to turn off Do Not Disturb or Focus:

1. Access your iPhone‚Äôs**Control Center** . The method to [open the Control Center](http://www.makeuseof.com/how-to-use-customize-control-center-iphone/) may vary depending on your iPhone model.
2. Tap**Do Not Disturb: On** (moon icon) to turn it off. Or tap any other Focus mode that is currently enabled to disable it.

## 5\. Check Your iPhone‚Äôs Sound Settings

2. Scroll to the**Sounds** **and Vibration Patterns** section.
3. Tap into each tone setting to check that there‚Äôs an alert tone selected. For example, tap**Text Tone** .
